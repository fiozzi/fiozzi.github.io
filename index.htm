Short Bio

Fabrizio Iozzi has been a Senior Lecturer in Mathematics and Computer Science at Bocconi since 2013. Fabrizio has a Master Degree in Mathematics (University of Milan, 1989) and has taught Mathematics, Computer Science and Computer Programming at undergraduate and graduate level since 1990 at Politecnico of Milan and from 2000 at Universit√† Bocconi.

At Bocconi, Fabrizio coordinated the development and the research activities in the field of technology enhanced learning and from September 2013 to September 2016 has been Lab Manager at BELSS, Bocconi Experimental Laboratory for the Social Sciences. From 2016 until 2022 Fabrizio was assistant to the director of the BEMACS udnergraduate program. He is currently assistant to the director (prof. Luca Trevisan) of the Master program in Artificial Intelligence at Bocconi.
